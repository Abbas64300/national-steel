About National Steel

National Steel is built on a simple principle — deliver technically correct products, with dependable supply and transparent communication. We work closely with contractors, procurement teams and engineers to ensure that each wire mesh solution meets functional requirements and site conditions. Our focus is not volume, but reliability, repeat business and long-term partnerships.

Our Core Specialisation

Stainless Steel Wire Mesh

Plain woven, crimped and welded SS mesh in grades 304, 316 and 310 for filtration, screening and industrial separation applications.

Welded Wire Mesh

GI, MS and SS welded mesh panels and rolls for structural reinforcement, safety fencing and industrial enclosures.

Chain Link & Barbed Wire

Precision-manufactured chain link fencing and IS-standard barbed wire for infrastructure, security and perimeter applications.

Perforated Sheets & Extrusion Screens

Round, square and slot perforated sheets along with extrusion screens manufactured to controlled open area, thickness and tolerance.
